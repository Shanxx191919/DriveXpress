Payment Options

Choose from our trusted payment methods to finance your dream car with ease and confidence.

BDO Auto Loan

Finance your car with BDO's flexible auto loan options. Enjoy competitive interest rates and a seamless application process.

  • Interest Rate: 5.5% per annum (fixed for 1-5 years)
  • Loan Term: Up to 5 years
  • Requirements:
    • Valid government-issued ID
    • Proof of income (e.g., ITR, payslips)
    • Certificate of Employment (if employed)
    • Minimum monthly income of ₱30,000
    • Downpayment: 20% of the car's price
  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days

BPI Auto Loan

Drive away with BPI's auto loan, offering low interest rates and fast approval for your car purchase.

  • Interest Rate: 5.8% per annum (fixed for 1-6 years)
  • Loan Term: Up to 6 years
  • Requirements:
    • Two valid IDs (government-issued)
    • Proof of income (e.g., BIR Form 2316, bank statements)
    • Latest utility bill (for address verification)
    • Minimum monthly income of ₱25,000
    • Downpayment: 15% of the car's price
  • Processing Time: 2-4 business days

GCash GLoan

Use GCash GLoan for a quick and convenient way to pay for your car with flexible installment plans.

  • Interest Rate: 1.5% per month (18% per annum)
  • Loan Term: Up to 24 months
  • Requirements:
    • Active GCash account with a good GScore
    • Verified GCash account (fully verified with ID)
    • No existing unpaid GLoans
    • Loan amount: Up to ₱125,000 (based on GScore)
    • No downpayment required for small loans
  • Processing Time: Instant approval upon eligibility

Shipping Methods

We offer reliable shipping options to ensure your car arrives safely and on time. Choose the method that suits you best.

K Line Shipping

K Line offers international shipping with a focus on safety and efficiency, ensuring your car arrives in top condition.

  • Procedure:
    • Book your shipping slot through our website or contact our support team.
    • Prepare your car for shipping (clean, remove personal items, ensure fuel tank is 1/4 full).
    • Drop off your car at the designated port or arrange for pickup (additional fee applies).
    • Track your shipment online using the provided tracking number.
    • Pick up your car at the destination port or opt for door-to-door delivery (if available).
  • Estimated Delivery Time: 7-14 days (depending on destination)
  • Cost: Starts at ₱50,000 (varies by distance and car size)
  • Important Information:
    • Customs clearance and duties are the responsibility of the customer.
    • Insurance is included for damages during transit (up to ₱500,000).
    • Required documents: Car title, bill of sale, export permit, and valid ID.
    • Ports served: Major international ports (e.g., Manila, Los Angeles, Tokyo).

Manila Forwarder

Manila Forwarder provides reliable domestic and international shipping with personalized customer service since 1998.

  • Procedure:
    • Contact Manila Forwarder via their website (www.manilaforwarder.com) or our support team to schedule shipping.
    • Prepare your car (remove personal items, ensure battery is disconnected, fuel tank 1/4 full).
    • Drop off your car at their Manila facility or arrange for pickup (available in Metro Manila).
    • Receive a tracking number to monitor your shipment's progress.
    • Collect your car at the destination or opt for home delivery (additional fee applies).
  • Estimated Delivery Time: 5-10 days (domestic), 10-20 days (international)
  • Cost: Starts at ₱30,000 for domestic, ₱60,000 for international
  • Important Information:
    • Customs fees and taxes are the customer's responsibility for international shipments.
    • Optional insurance available for purchase (covers up to ₱1,000,000).
    • Required documents: Car registration, proof of ownership, and valid ID.
    • Specializes in Balikbayan services; offers discounts for multiple shipments.

NEX Worldwide Express

NEX Worldwide Express provides fast and secure global shipping, ensuring your car reaches its destination with ease.

  • Procedure:
    • Schedule your shipment through our website or NEX's customer service.
    • Prepare your car for shipping (remove personal items, ensure fuel tank is 1/4 full, secure loose parts).
    • Drop off your car at a NEX facility or arrange for pickup (available in major cities).
    • Use the tracking number provided to monitor your shipment in real-time.
    • Receive your car at the destination port or opt for door-to-door delivery (additional fee applies).
  • Estimated Delivery Time: 5-12 days (depending on destination)
  • Cost: Starts at ₱55,000 (varies by distance and car size)
  • Important Information:
    • Customs clearance and duties are the customer's responsibility.
    • Comprehensive insurance included (covers up to ₱750,000 for damages during transit).
    • Required documents: Car title, export permit, proof of ownership, and valid ID.
    • Offers express shipping for urgent deliveries (additional fee applies).

Security Measures & FAQs

Your transactions are secured with SSL encryption, ensuring your data is protected. Below are answers to common questions about buying a car online with DriveXpress.

At DriveXpress, we prioritize your security. All transactions are processed using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption, which ensures that your payment details, such as credit card numbers or bank information, are transmitted securely. We also comply with PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) to protect your data. Additionally, we do not store your full payment information on our servers; it is handled directly by our trusted payment partners (BDO, BPI, and GCash).

We understand the importance of trust when buying a car online. Every car listed on DriveXpress undergoes a thorough inspection by our certified mechanics to ensure it meets our quality standards. We provide a detailed inspection report for each vehicle, including mileage, engine condition, and any repairs needed. Additionally, all cars come with a verified title and ownership history to prevent fraud. You can also request a virtual tour or in-person inspection before finalizing your purchase.

We offer a 7-day return policy for your peace of mind. If the car does not match the description provided or has undisclosed issues, you can initiate a return within 7 days of delivery. To qualify, the car must be in the same condition as when delivered (no additional damage or mileage over 100km). Contact our support team to start the return process. Note that shipping costs for returns are the customer's responsibility unless the issue is due to our error. Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days after the car is returned and inspected.

Delivery times depend on your chosen shipping method and location. With K Line Shipping, expect 7-14 days for international deliveries; Manila Forwarder offers 5-10 days for domestic and 10-20 days for international; NEX Worldwide Express delivers in 5-12 days. Once your payment is confirmed, we’ll provide a tracking number so you can monitor your shipment in real-time. Please ensure all required documents (e.g., car title, export permit) are submitted to avoid delays. Customs clearance for international shipments may add 2-5 days, depending on the destination country.

To purchase a car through DriveXpress, you’ll need to provide the following documents: a valid government-issued ID (e.g., passport, driver’s license), proof of address (e.g., utility bill), and proof of income if applying for financing (e.g., ITR, payslips, or bank statements). For international buyers, an export permit may be required depending on the destination country. If you’re using a loan (e.g., BDO or BPI Auto Loan), additional documents like a Certificate of Employment or BIR Form 2316 may be needed. All documents must be submitted digitally during the checkout process.

We strive for transparency in all our transactions. The price you see for the car includes the base cost, but additional fees may apply, such as shipping costs (₱30,000-₱60,000 depending on the method), taxes (5-15% of the car price, calculated at checkout), and a processing fee (₱5,000). For international buyers, customs duties and clearance fees are your responsibility and vary by country. All fees will be clearly outlined in your cart before you confirm your purchase, so there are no surprises.

Privacy Policy

At DriveXpress, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Below is an overview of how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

What is a privacy policy?

A privacy policy is a legal document where we disclose what data we collect from users, how we manage the collected data, and how we use that data. The important objective of a privacy policy is to inform users how we collect, use, and manage the collected data.

Why is a privacy policy important?

The most important thing to remember is that a privacy policy is required by law if we collect data from users, either directly or indirectly. For example, if you submit a contact form on our website, we need a privacy policy. We also need a privacy policy because we use analytics tools such as Google Analytics to understand user behavior and improve our services.

Where do I put my privacy policy?

You can find our privacy policy here, in an easy-to-find location on our website. We recommend placing the privacy policy in a prominent location, such as the footer of the website, but we’ve included it as a dedicated section for clarity.

What does the privacy policy include?

Our privacy policy includes: what data we collect from visitors (e.g., name, email, phone number, IP address), how we collect it (e.g., through forms, cookies, or analytics tools), why we are collecting the data (e.g., to process orders, improve our website), and how we are using the data (e.g., to fulfill orders, send promotional emails with consent, or analyze user behavior).

How do we protect your data?

We use industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, to protect your data during transmission. Our servers are secured with firewalls and access controls to prevent unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and while we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

What are your rights and choices?

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us at support@drivexpress.com. You can also opt out of marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email we send. We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the Data Privacy Act of 2012 in the Philippines, and you may request a copy of the data we hold about you.

Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Below are the details of our refund policy to ensure a fair and transparent process.

What is our return window?

We offer a 7-day return window for all car purchases. You may initiate a return within 7 days from the date of delivery if the car does not match the description provided or has undisclosed defects. This policy applies to both domestic and international purchases.

What are the conditions for a return?

To be eligible for a return, the car must be in the same condition as when delivered, with no additional damage or modifications. The mileage should not exceed 100km beyond what was recorded at delivery. You must also provide proof of purchase (e.g., order confirmation email) and all original documentation that came with the car.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, contact our support team at support@drivexpress.com or through our Contact page within 7 days of delivery. Provide your order number, a description of the issue, and photos of the car if applicable. We will review your request and provide instructions for returning the car, including the shipping address and any required documentation.

Who covers the return shipping costs?

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., wrong car delivered, undisclosed major defects). In such cases, we will cover the return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping method, as we are not responsible for items lost during return transit.

How long does the refund process take?

Once we receive and inspect the returned car, we will process your refund within 5-10 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please note that shipping fees, taxes, and processing fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
